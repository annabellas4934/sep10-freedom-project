# Entry 6
##### 05/11/26

## Context:
We have finally started working on our freedom project, using everything we learned within the past year. We were given a week to finish our MVP. When I started I struggled implementing Bulma with Bootstrap because I had to work with them separately, but not together, and they had similar classes that conflicted with each other. At first, I tried to use only Bulma, but then I realized that there were components in Bootstrap that I needed for my project to look like how I wanted for my wireframe. I looked it up and according to the [Github community](https://github.com/jgthms/bulma/issues/1865) it works but with different classes.according to [Bulma’s site](https://bulma.io/alternative-to-bootstrap/) it can serve as a good alternative. I did use a lot of Bulma’s content for my website, but bootstrap was much simpler for the grid system. Eventually I came up with an MVP, though I’m not satisfied with it, it serves its purpose.

## Skills:
* Time management: While needing to work for my MVP, I also had to deal with rehearsals as well as the actual day of the musical that I’ve been working on for my extracurricular in theater. It was tough managing the project with a show upcoming due to rehearsals ending at 5:30 making me go home by six. I had very limited time to work on my homework that was due the next day. Also the show being on the weekend made it difficult to work on the project because that’s when I would probably be doing most of my work. Most of the work I’ve done for my project was done in class. I use the most of that time not allowing myself to get distracted with friends. This helped me a lot as I had very few things needed to be done on Sunday for my MVP.

* Embracing failure: My MVP is not satisfactory, but I can go beyond the MVP in the later week. I was pressed for time and needed something that would work for my MVP. From what it looks like, my project does look similar to what I wanted for my wireframe. Now that I do have an MVP and I have more time, I can add CSS and change a little more formatting so that it does look much better.

## Engineering Design Process:
I'm currently on the creation step of the project, having just finished my MVP. Now I am working towards a beyond MVP, and starting to get ready for presentation.
